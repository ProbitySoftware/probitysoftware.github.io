Conference Overview

Corporate Farmers International and International Institute of Tropical Agriculture (IITA)

Organiser of Agri Social Media Week 2018 (7 - 8 February)
The Corporate Farmers International, an advocacy group on Agribusiness Development in Nigeria and Africa with innovation is pleased to invite you to be part of Agri Social media week 2018 to Talk, Do and Create innovative ideas that will develop and grow Nigeria’s agricultural sector.
As you are aware, the Government of Nigeria has pledged its commitment to promoting the development of the Nigerian agricultural sector as part of its overall plan to diversify the Nigerian economy and boost revenue earnings currently impacted by falling oil prices.
This Agri Social Media Week is aimed at supporting government commitment to agricultural development by promoting dialogue and partnership with both local and international stakeholders including national governments, investors, businesses, diplomatic community, and the civil society on this very important commitment.


Programs for the Agribusiness Social Media week
  • Day 1: Fintech Disruption in Agribusiness and Branding
  • Day 2: Climate change and way forward and Agriculture Finance
